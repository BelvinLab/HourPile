# HourPile – Project Charter

## Project Vision

Many people dream of learning a new language, acquiring technical skills, or mastering a new field. However, for most learners, the greatest challenge is not motivation—motivation is temporary. The real challenge is maintaining discipline and consistency over months or even years.

This realization came from my own experience while learning English independently. Despite having a strong desire to improve, I often found it difficult to measure my progress and stay consistent over time. Existing applications mainly focus on teaching content but rarely provide a simple and comprehensive way to track the entire learning journey.

HourPile was born from this need.

The project aims to provide learners with a platform where they can record every learning session, monitor their progress through meaningful statistics, build their personal vocabulary, and stay committed to their long-term goals.

Rather than simply helping users learn, HourPile seeks to help them **keep learning**.

## Mission

To empower lifelong learners by providing a simple, reliable, and data-driven platform that encourages discipline, consistency, and measurable progress throughout their learning journey.

## Problem Statement

Many learners abandon their learning goals because they lose consistency over time.

The absence of clear progress indicators and long-term tracking often makes learners feel that they are not improving, even when they are.

HourPile addresses this problem by allowing users to record their learning activities, visualize their progress, and develop sustainable learning habits.

## Initial Scope (MVP)

The first version of HourPile focuses on the core features required to validate the product:

* User authentication
* Language management
* Learning session tracking
* Personal vocabulary notebook
* Learning statistics dashboard

The MVP intentionally excludes social features in order to validate the core value proposition as quickly as possible.

## Long-Term Vision

HourPile is designed to evolve beyond a personal tracking application into a collaborative learning platform.

Future versions will introduce:

* learning goals and streaks;
* intelligent vocabulary revision;
* resource sharing;
* discussion groups;
* community challenges;
* social interactions;
* AI-powered personalized recommendations.

The long-term ambition is to build a global community where learners support each other, stay accountable, and achieve their learning goals together.

## Target Users

* Language learners
* Self-taught learners
* University students
* Professionals pursuing continuous learning
* Anyone wishing to build consistent learning habits

## Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if it enables users to:

* maintain a consistent learning routine;
* visualize their progress over time;
* remain motivated through measurable achievements;
* build lasting learning habits.

Ultimately, HourPile aims to become a trusted companion for lifelong learning rather than just another educational application.
